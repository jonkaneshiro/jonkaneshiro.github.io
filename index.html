Live your best life.

Our wrist-worn Heart Watch wearable has been clinically validated to continuously measure heart rate and heart rate variability (HRV) to quantitatively measure stress, cardio-respiratory fitness (via percentage of your max volume of oxygen consumption), cardiovascular performance and irregular heart rhythms.

We have successfully integrated our Heart Watch technology into a remote patient monitoring system, enabling physicians to monitor their patient's stress, weight, blood pressure and heart rhythms, between office visits. Through personalized care plans and higher means of communication, LIVMOR is on a mission to prevent, detect and manage chronic conditions, in real-time and non-invasively with health information electronically and securely transfered to the patient's provider, caregiver and other designated people.
